## Title

First public Audio-to-MIDI beta workflow

## Body

This update makes the first Audio-to-MIDI workflow clearer for early users:

- the official conversion service is selected automatically;
- users do not choose or configure a backend;
- upload, queue, processing, done, failed, and stopped-waiting states are visible;
- generated MIDI can be downloaded or opened directly in MIDI Piano Trainer;
- bad conversion feedback has a dedicated GitHub issue route.

Audio-to-MIDI is still experimental. Clean short piano recordings work best, and noisy/full-song audio may produce wrong notes. Real bad-conversion examples are useful for improving quality.
