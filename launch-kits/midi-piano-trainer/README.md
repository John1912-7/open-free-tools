## One-Line Pitch

Free open-source browser MIDI piano trainer with an experimental audio-to-MIDI workflow.

## Short Pitch

MIDI Piano Trainer lets you upload a MIDI file, see falling notes, listen to the built-in piano sound, choose tracks, and practice in the browser. The Audio-to-MIDI beta can turn short clean piano recordings into MIDI, then open the result directly in the trainer.

## Honest Beta Disclaimer

Audio-to-MIDI is experimental. Clean short piano recordings work best. Noisy recordings, full songs, heavy reverb, or multiple instruments may produce wrong notes.
